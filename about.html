About

Father Kenneth Allen is a Catholic priest, spiritual director, and writer exploring the mandate for creativity, prosperity, and the vocation of the human person, rooted in Sacred Scripture. His history is in Sacred Music and the arts.

He is incardinated in the Archdiocese of New Orleans, currently on a medical leave of absence.

His friends have been repeatedly telling him, for several years, to rid himself of his WordPress site. "It's easy!" they say, as they code away all the day. We're getting there.
